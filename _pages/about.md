I am an undergraduate student at [Seoul National University](https://en.snu.ac.kr), double majoring in [computer science](https://cse.snu.ac.kr/en) and [mathematics](https://www.math.snu.ac.kr).

My research interests lie in **3D computer vision** and **generative models**, particularly in generating large and complex 3D scenes. Ultimately, I aim to develop machine learning systems capable of understanding and creating rich **physical 3D environments** for applications such as simulations, games, and animations.

I am currently conducting research with Prof. [Jaesik Park](https://jaesik.info) at Seoul National University. Previously, I worked with Prof. [Ernest K. Ryu](https://ernestryu.com) at UCLA.
